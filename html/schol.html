Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana

The Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) is a crop insurance scheme launched in 2016 to protect farmers from financial losses due to natural calamities, pests, and diseases. It covers crop losses and shares the premium cost between the government and farmers, reducing their vulnerability and stabilizing the agricultural sector.

Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchai Yojana

The Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchai Yojana (PMKSY) is a centrally sponsored scheme launched in 2015 to support farmers in installing micro-irrigation systems. It provides financial assistance through interest-free loans for the first two years, benefiting farmers with less than 2 hectares of land. The scheme improves water use efficiency, boosts crop production, and enhances farmers' income, promoting sustainable agriculture.

Krishi Kalyan Abhiyan

Krishi Kalyan Abhiyan (KKA): Launched in 2018, KKA is a centrally sponsored scheme targeting aspirational districts to enhance farmers' income and livelihoods through activities like soil health card distribution, livestock vaccination, and organic farming promotion. The scheme has trained over 11 lakh farmers and improved soil health on 15 lakh hectares of land.
